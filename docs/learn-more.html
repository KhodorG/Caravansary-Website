Core Focus Areas

Trade Facilitation

  • Establishing and managing Caravansary Trade Centers that serves as hubs for import/export activities.
  • Supporting businesses in navigating cross-border trade, logistics, and regulatory requirements.
  • Creating platforms for B2B connections, fostering partnerships between suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors in the regions we serve.

Industrial Development

  • Developing and managing Caravansary Industrial Parks, providing infrastructure for manufacturing, warehousing, and logistics.
  • Promoting industrial collaboration between West African and Turkish companies to enhance value chains and drive economic growth.

Tourism and MICE Industry

  • Developing Caravansary MICE Cities (Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, and Exhibitions) to attract business and leisure tourism.
  • Focusing on the bleisure (business + leisure) industry by creating integrated spaces for work, networking, and relaxation.

Fund Management

  • Raising and managing investment funds.
  • Structuring and deploying capital into high-impact projects with long-term revenue potential.

Geographic and Economic Scope

Geographic Focus: Facilitating economic integration and trade between West Africa and Turkey, leveraging strategic geographic and cultural ties.

Economic Impact: Driving sustainable development, job creation, and economic diversification in both regions.

Key Services

  • Project Development: Identifying, planning, and executing large-scale infrastructure and trade-related projects.
  • Investment Facilitation: Attracting investors and structuring investment vehicles for high-growth opportunities.
  • Operational Management: Overseeing the development and ongoing management of trade centers, industrial parks, and tourism hubs.
  • Advisory Services: Providing expertise in market entry, regulatory compliance, and trade optimization for businesses.

Strategic Vision

Caravansary Capital positions itself as a bridge between economies, fostering collaboration, innovation, and growth through:

  • Leveraging its deep understanding of regional markets.
  • Building infrastructure that supports trade, industry, and tourism.
  • Creating sustainable revenue streams while generating value for investors and local communities.
