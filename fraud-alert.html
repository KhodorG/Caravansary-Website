Fraud Alert

FRAUD ALERT WARNING

Caravansary Capital is committed to conducting business with the highest standards of integrity and transparency. We take fraud prevention seriously and wish to alert our partners, investors, and the general public about potential fraudulent activities misusing our name, brand, and reputation.

Beware of Fraudulent Activities

We have received reports of unauthorized individuals and entities falsely claiming to represent Caravansary Capital. These fraudulent activities may include but are not limited to:

  • Fake investment offers promising unrealistic returns.
  • Unsolicited emails, phone calls, or messages requesting personal information or financial transactions.
  • Fraudulent websites, social media accounts, or advertisements misusing our name, logo, and branding.
  • Impersonation of Caravansary Capital executives or representatives.

How to Identify Legitimate Communication from Caravansary Capital

To protect yourself from fraud, please note the following:

  • Official communications from Caravansary Capital will only come from our verified domain: [Insert Official Website URL].
  • We do not solicit investments through social media, unsolicited phone calls, or unofficial email addresses.
  • Any investment opportunity, partnership proposal, or financial transaction must be formally documented and legally vetted.
  • Our official representatives will never request personal banking information, payments, or confidential details via informal channels.

Steps to Protect Yourself

If you suspect fraudulent activity, take the following precautions:

  • Verify Authenticity: Contact us directly through our official website or office before responding to any suspicious communication.
  • Do Not Share Personal Information: Never disclose your financial details, passwords, or personal information to unverified sources.
  • Report Suspicious Activity: If you receive a suspicious request or encounter unauthorized use of our brand, please report it immediately to [Insert Contact Email or Hotline].
  • Stay Informed: Regularly check our official website for updates on fraud prevention and alerts.

Caravansary Capital’s Liability Disclaimer

Caravansary Capital is not responsible for any losses incurred due to fraudulent activities carried out by unauthorized third parties misusing our name or brand. We urge all stakeholders to exercise caution and verify any claims before engaging in financial transactions.
