Caravansary Capital Policy Handbook

Last Updated: 01, May, 2025

1. Introduction

Caravansary Capital is committed to upholding the highest ethical, legal, and professional standards across all areas of our business. This Policy Handbook serves as a comprehensive guide to our governance, compliance, sustainability, and operational principles.

2. Governance & Compliance

Ethics & Compliance Statement

We adhere to the principles of integrity, transparency, and accountability in all business dealings. Our corporate culture promotes ethical behavior and legal compliance at every level.

Anti-Bribery & Corruption Policy

Caravansary Capital enforces a strict zero-tolerance policy on bribery and corruption. Employees, partners, and suppliers must adhere to anti-corruption laws and ethical business practices.

Whistleblower Policy

We encourage employees and stakeholders to report unethical practices confidentially and without fear of retaliation.

Conflict of Interest Policy

All employees, executives, and partners must disclose any conflicts of interest that could compromise business integrity.

3. Business Conduct & Investment Standards

Investor Relations Disclosure

We provide transparent and accurate financial and operational information to our investors, ensuring full compliance with disclosure regulations.

Responsible Investment & ESG Guidelines

Caravansary Capital integrates Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) principles into our investment decisions, ensuring sustainable and ethical business growth.

Supplier Code of Conduct

Our suppliers and partners must adhere to ethical labor practices, sustainability commitments, and fair business standards.

4. Corporate Social Responsibility & Sustainability

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Policy

We are committed to making a positive impact on society through community engagement, philanthropy, and sustainable development.

Sustainability Policy

We prioritize eco-friendly business operations, responsible resource management, and investments that contribute to environmental conservation.

Human Rights Policy

Caravansary Capital upholds international human rights standards and ensures fair and ethical treatment of employees and stakeholders.

5. Workplace & Employee Guidelines

Diversity, Equity & Inclusion (DEI) Policy

We foster an inclusive workplace that values diversity and equal opportunity for all employees.

Workplace Safety & Well-being

We are committed to ensuring a safe, healthy, and productive work environment for all employees and partners.

Employee Code of Conduct

All employees are expected to uphold the highest ethical and professional standards in their conduct and interactions.

6. Digital & Customer Protection

Privacy Policy

We respect data privacy and comply with regulations to protect customer and stakeholder information.

Terms & Conditions

Our website and services are governed by clear terms that define user rights, responsibilities, and limitations.

Fraud Alert

We actively monitor and protect against fraudulent activities, providing guidelines on how stakeholders can report suspicious activities.

Website Accessibility Statement

Caravansary Capital is committed to ensuring our digital platforms are accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
